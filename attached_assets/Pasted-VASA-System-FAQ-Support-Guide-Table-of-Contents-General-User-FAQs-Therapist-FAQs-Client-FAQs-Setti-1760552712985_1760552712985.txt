General User FAQs
Account & Access
Q: How do I reset my password? A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link via email. The system uses secure email verification for password resets.

Q: Why can't I log in to my account? A: Common reasons include:

Incorrect username or password (passwords are case-sensitive)
Account may need email verification (check your inbox)
Browser cache issues (try clearing cache or using incognito mode)
Ensure you're selecting the correct user type (Individual, Client, or Therapist)
Q: Can I use VASA on multiple devices? A: Yes, VASA features cross-device sync. You can access the platform from any device with a web browser. The responsive design works on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Your session history and progress sync automatically across all devices.

Q: What browsers work best with VASA? A: VASA currently supports:

Chrome (desktop and mobile)
Firefox (desktop and mobile)
Safari (desktop and mobile)
Edge (desktop and mobile) The platform uses WebSocket technology for real-time features, so ensure JavaScript is enabled.
Voice Sessions & AI Agents
Q: Which AI voice agents are available? A: VASA offers four specialized AI therapeutic agents:

Sarah: Empathetic and nurturing companion
Marcus: Performance-focused motivational coach
Elena Wise: Grounded, naturally maternal with life experience
Mathew: PCA/PCP methodology specialist with Master PCA analysis
Q: Do the AI agents remember our previous conversations? A: Yes! All AI agents feature memory persistence. They remember your previous conversations, personal details, and can reference past sessions to provide continuity in your therapeutic journey.

Q: How long are my voice sessions recorded and stored? A: Voice sessions are automatically transcribed and stored securely with complete context preservation. You can access your full session history, including AI-generated summaries and insights, through your dashboard.

Cancellation & Refunds
Q: How do I cancel my subscription? A: Easy Cancellation Process:

Click the Settings gear icon in the navigation header
Select "Cancel Subscription" from the dropdown menu
Confirm cancellation in the dialog that appears
Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period
You'll receive a confirmation email
Q: What happens when I cancel? A: Upon cancellation:

Immediate: Cancellation is processed, no future charges
Until period ends: Full access to all features continues
After period ends:
Voice sessions become unavailable
Historical data remains accessible (read-only)
You can resubscribe at any time to restore full access
Clients: If you're a therapist, clients lose voice session access when your subscription ends
Q: Can I get a refund? A: Refund policies:

Trial cancellations: No charges applied during trial period
Monthly subscriptions: Pro-rated refunds may be available for recent charges
One-time purchases: Refunds considered on case-by-case basis
Contact support for refund requests and specific circumstances
Q: How do I reactivate a cancelled subscription? A: To reactivate:

Go to Settings > Subscription
Select "Reactivate Subscription"
Choose your desired plan
Complete payment setup
Access restores immediately upon successful payment

Check your internet connection (stable connection required)
Restart your browser or device
Try using headphones with a built-in microphone

Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
Disable VPN if using one
Try switching to a different device or browser
Check for browser updates
Clear browser cache and cookies
Q: Can I use VASA on my phone? A: Yes! VASA mobile support includes:
